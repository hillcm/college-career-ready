Cydney's College Pro Tips

Go to class


Even if you dont want to, fall into good habits.

Study Everyday

Develop good study habits. Try studying for about 3-4 hours everyday to build your knowledge on course material. Find study buddies
it is okay if your friends are not your study buddies.

Manage your time

Get a physical planner or use gCal to plan your days out. In your planner put in reminders of projects, papers, and exam dates.
in your planner write down all your classes for the semester then you will be able to see where you time is going to plan studying and extra curriculars. in your put in reminders of prject/paper/ exam dates.

Check your email!

This is the way professors and the way the rest of the university communicates with you. A professor might email you and say that class is canceled.

Utilize Campus resources

Reach out to your academic advisors, go to the gym, visit the museums, and join orgs on campus.

Don't be
afraid to fail

There is failure in all stories of success. Learn from your mistakes and move on.

Be open to learn

Be open to learn in class and from other students who are different than you. Become comfortbale with being uncomfortable.

Get to know your instructors

Go to office hours to get to know them. Ask them how there day was. If they can attach a name to a face they might help you when you are between a C+ and B-.
